# Phase 04 - Scope Decision

## Goal

Resolve only the decisions required before selected extraction.

## Keep in context

- `questions.md`
- `policies/questions.md`
- `BUILDPRINT_CANDIDATES.md`

## Steps

- Ask at most one blocking question in chat at a time.
- Confirm selected candidate/system path.
- Confirm production-grade selected scope.
- Confirm fidelity/proof target and provider/export/live parity boundaries.
- Move non-blocking unknowns to appendix.

## Do not

- Ask a long questionnaire.
- Ask questions the repo already answered.
- Let the user accidentally choose broad fake coverage.

## Exit criteria

- Candidate/scope/fidelity are confirmed or blocker is recorded.
- Excluded capabilities are listed instead of faked.

## Validation evidence

- `questions.md` and final handover record decisions and assumptions.
