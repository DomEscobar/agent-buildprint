# SOURCE_SURFACE_COVERAGE

This file is the source-surface loss detector for the Toonflow App full-suite selected Buildprint. It records high-signal source surfaces from `https://github.com/HBAI-Ltd/Toonflow-app` at commit `122d2aa431d3240fea3eab491e6fbc690bb088cb` and their selected-output disposition.

Source surfaces are evidence, not requirements. This Buildprint does not require route/function/file parity and does not ask the implementation agent to clone Toonflow's source structure. The invariant is that source-backed product obligations survive mapping: every high-signal surface must be owned by a capability, intentionally merged into a capability, explicitly user-excluded, or blocked.

## Disposition Legend

- `OWNED_BY_CAPABILITY`: surface supports a named capability and product obligation.
- `MERGED_INTO_CAPABILITY`: surface is intentionally absorbed into a broader capability boundary.
- `OUT_OF_SCOPE_BY_USER_ONLY`: surface is excluded only by explicit user decision or selected-target boundary.
- `BLOCKED_NEEDS_REVIEW`: surface is high-signal but cannot yet be safely mapped.
- `LOW_SIGNAL_IGNORED_WITH_REASON`: surface was downgraded with evidence and is not product-relevant.
