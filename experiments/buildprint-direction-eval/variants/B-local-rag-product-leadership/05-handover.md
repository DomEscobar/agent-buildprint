# Handover

Write a short honest handover at the end.

Use these headings:

## Current status

Does the local RAG loop run? What remains prototype-level?

## Built surfaces

List library/ingestion, retrieval, grounded chat, citations, history/memory, settings/provider boundaries. Mark built/partial/blocked/not started.

## Verification

Commands run, manual/browser paths checked, screenshots or artifact paths.

## Known defects and blockers

Concrete user-visible issues and next repair.

## Next atomic actions

Numbered next steps a human or agent can take without rereading the whole packet.
