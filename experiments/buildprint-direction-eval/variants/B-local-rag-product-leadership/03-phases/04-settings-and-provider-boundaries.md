# Phase 04 — Settings and Provider Boundaries

## Product intention

The user understands what is local, what is optional live AI, and what is blocked until configured.

## Build

- Provider/status panel.
- Retrieval settings that actually affect results.
- Export/delete/reset controls with safe confirmation.
- Missing credential states for live LLM/embeddings.

## Quality bar

Settings must either work or be honestly disabled. Destructive actions must be safe.

## Do not ship

Fake live mode, silent no-op settings, destructive controls without confirmation, or provider marketing copy.
