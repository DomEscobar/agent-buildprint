# Final Reviewer

Before handover, become skeptical.

Review the app before reading/writing explanations:

- Add two different documents.
- Ask a question answerable by one document and one not answerable by either.
- Verify citations/snippets change with the query.
- Reload and verify documents/history persist.
- Click upload, ask, source, settings, reset/export controls.
- Look for generic chatbot smell, fake citations, canned answer, hidden retrieval, raw JSON, dead controls, and fake live provider success.

Fix local, safe, central defects. Leave only real blockers.
