# Final Reviewer

Before handover, become hostile to the product.

Review the app before reading or writing explanations:

- Start fresh and complete the core loop.
- Reload and verify meaningful state returns.
- Change seed/config and verify output changes.
- Click every visible primary control.
- Trigger empty/error/blocked-provider states where possible.
- Look for generic dashboard smell, dead controls, raw JSON, placeholder copy, canned output, fake intelligence, and non-persistent state.

Fix local, safe, central defects. Leave blockers only when they are real: credentials, destructive actions, deployment, security, large scope, or environment failure.
