# Acceptance Scope

The slice includes:

- First-run guided screen.
- Paste notes or load example.
- Analyze notes button.
- Plain-language result: people, topics, risks, suggested next step.
- Details section with advanced ontology/provider/export facts.
- Local persistence/readback.
- JSON export.

Out of scope:

- Real OpenAI/Zep/OASIS calls.
- Multi-user auth.
- Production deployment.
